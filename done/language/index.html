Language

Fri 16 November 2018
By ixqifeba
Language is the limit of description; of our grasp. Cognition is the train running in its tracks. They are one; the frozen and the fluid. Let these not illude and stand in for (belie) what is. There are few, if any, poems about the Pancreas, despite importance, because like language we only see thrill and panic, not the substrate. Our reliance on it for perception is but a metaphor for the grandeur that awaits without it.
