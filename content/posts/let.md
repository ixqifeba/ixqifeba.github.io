Title: Let
Date: Oct 10 2018
Status: published
Slug: let
Summary: 

<div class="post-poem">
Let the flower,
be the flower.

Let the wind,
be the wind.

Let the wave,
be the wave.

Let the emotion,
be the emotion.

Not a sign,
nor a totem,
or anything
but itself.

The world 
does not exist
simply to 
inform you.
</div>
