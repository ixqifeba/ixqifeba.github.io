Title: Let
Date: Oct 10
Category: Poems
Slug: let
Summary: 

<div class="post-poem">
Let the wind 
be the wind.
--
Let the wave
be the wave.

Let the emotion
be the emotion.

Let the mood
be the mood.

--/

Not a sign,
or a totem,
or anything else
but itself.

--
The world 
does not exist
to inform you.
</div>
