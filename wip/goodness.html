Goodness

You must remember that in the deepest time of need, that there is goodness in you. You may not see it now, but know that it is there, behind overcast skies. (??) It is there in the throes and the fits, despair and elation, willed or otherwise. Beware though, it does not license your acts, nor condone your crimes, nor to allay concerns. It is not of these trivialities, but much deeper. It is there in the way you face your hurt despite the hurt. It is there in how you cry for it to be there (for the simplest things) beyond anything else. It is there because none of this dance would be possible if the core wasn’t good. (if it wasn’t) Also remember, that it is there in everyone else.
