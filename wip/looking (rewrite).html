Looking (to be rewritten)

Thu 18 October 2018

(So complete that I fell asleep) Punching wildly across the veil, and against the shadows from a forgotten slumber. — To look, and let be. Forestalling a growth, even when it undoes no pain, as I hoped, but forestalls the encounter with the unfurling of a beautiful song. nb: song is not fitting the unfurling The great crime committed is to not let be, be what is.
